Understanding Your Camera:

Basic Camera Settings

Your camera is your gateway to wildlife photography. Understanding its basic functions is crucial for capturing those perfect moments in nature.

Shooting Modes
  • Manual Mode: Complete control over your settings
  • Aperture Priority (A/Av): Control depth of field
  • Shutter Priority (S/Tv): Perfect for moving wildlife
  • Program Mode: When you need quick shots

The Exposure Triangle
